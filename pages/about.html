Background

Founded in 2011, the Uganda Alcohol Policy Alliance (UAPA) is a network of civil society organizations (CSO) working to reduce alcohol related harm in Uganda through advocacy for effective policies and making positive contributions working with Ministry of Health (Mental Health Division). UAPA operates at national level while the member organizations at district and community levels

Who We Are

Uganda Alcohol Policy Alliance (UAPA) is a national wide advocacy umbrella organization currently with 40 members working towards popularization of alcohol Control Policies and related activities in Uganda. A major anticipated outcome of UAPA is the enactment of the National Alcohol Control Policies and laws.

The primary target population of UAPA in all her efforts include but not limited to the policy makers, government ministries like Ministry of Health, Ministry of Trade and Industry, & Ministry of Education and Sports, and Uganda National Bureau of Standards (UNBS) and like-minded civil society organizations

We're Not An Island

UAPA is part of the East Africa Alcohol Policy Alliance Network which is a membership based organization with a management body comprising of elected members from the different member organisations. Being a voluntary network organisation, UAPA relies on a constitution for governance of members.

Uganda Alcohol Policy Alliance (UAPA) is a network of civil society organizations (CSO) that started in 2011, to advocate for regulation to minimise alcohol related harm in Uganda. Under UAPA umbrella members have united against alcohol abuse /misuse in the Country.

Mission

To provide a platform where individuals and civil society organizations can contribute to policy and processes on regulation of alcohol production, distribution and consumption so as to prevent alcohol related harm among theUgandan population.

Vision

A nation free from alcohol related harm. This is achieved by providing a platform where civil society is mobilized and empowered to demand for effective alcohol regulatory measures

Core Values

Transparency Uganda Alcohol Policy Alliance guarantees a transparent way of operating with a proper accountability system, which implies a responsible management of funding and community resources.

Objectives

To provide a platform for individuals and civil society organizations to influence policies and processes on regulation of alcohol manufacture, distribution and consumption to prevent alcohol related harm among the Ugandan population.

To carry out extensive education on the health and economic problems that affect the community with emphasis on alcohol, substance abuse and related areas.

To engage in the building of capacity of stakeholders in relation to promoting and delivering good policies and practices in the area of prevention and intervention on alcohol related harm.

To engage in the advocacy for the enactment of alcohol policies in Uganda that will regulate the production, distribution and consumption of alcohol.

To promote income generating projects so as to sustain the mission and works of the Company.
